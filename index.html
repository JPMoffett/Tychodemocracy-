Tychodemocracy

Democracy Through Random Selection



1. Foundational Principles

1. Random Selection (“Tychos”)

All legislative and administrative officials—at every level—are chosen randomly from the eligible population, similar to jury duty.

2. Third‑Generation Citizenship Requirement

Only citizens who can demonstrate at least three generations of continuous national citizenship are eligible for selection.

◦ This is meant to ensure deep civic roots and commitment.

3. Two‑Year Maximum Terms

◦ All officials serve no more than 2 years.

◦ No reappointment for at least 10 years.

◦ Their normal life expenses (housing, food, healthcare, utilities) are fully covered during service to prevent undue influence or bribery.

4. No Political Parties

◦ All parties, factions, or organized political blocs are banned.

◦ Governance is individual, civic, and duty‑bound—not partisan.

5. No Electoral College / No Districts

◦ The entire country is treated as one unified constituency.

◦ Random selection ensures natural demographic representation without gerrymandering.

6. Direct Digital Executive Election

◦ President + Two Vice Presidents are elected by universal direct vote.

◦ Voting is performed via secure phone/digital system using SSN verification.

◦ The top vote‑getter becomes President.
